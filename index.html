STEAM: PLAYER RETENTION
RECOMMENDER SYSTEM

This project analyzes Steam player behavior, game interactions, and user-generated reviews to predict player retention using machine learning. Leveraging a large-scale multi-modal dataset, we combine behavioral logs, textual data, and metadata to model factors that influence a player's ongoing activity.

INTRODUCTION

Our approach to player retention integrates supervised learning, error analysis, and a similarity-based recommendation system to predict churn, understand its underlying drivers, and identify competing games. We will be able to observe actionable insights into player engagement, personalization, and retention strategies in gaming ecosystems.
